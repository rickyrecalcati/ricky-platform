# Weekly Business Review

A weekly review template to help founders and managers stay focused on what matters most.

## How to use this

Complete this once per week, ideally every Friday afternoon or Monday morning. The goal is not to create more admin. The goal is to pause, review the truth, and decide what deserves attention next.

A good business is not managed by memory. It is managed by rhythm.
