# Business Health Scorecard

Evaluate your business across operations, finance, people and growth.

## How to use this

Use this once per month.

Score each area from 1 to 5.

1 = weak  
2 = inconsistent  
3 = acceptable  
4 = strong  
5 = excellent  

The goal is not to get perfect scores.

The goal is to identify where the business needs attention before problems become expensive.

## 8. Interpretation

25–30: Strong business  
You have a healthy foundation. Focus on refinement and growth.

19–24: Stable but exposed  
The business works, but several areas need attention before scaling.

13–18: Fragile  
The business may be relying too heavily on people, effort or luck.

Below 13: Urgent attention required  
Focus on stabilising the business before pursuing growth.
